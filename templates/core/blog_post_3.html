More Details

Mr. Setty, who was appointed managing director in January 2020, currently oversees SBI’s International Banking, Global Markets, and Technology verticals.

He will succeed Dinesh Kumar Khara, who is set to superannuate on August 28 upon reaching the upper age limit of 63 for the position of SBI Chairman.

Mr. Setty, who has also chaired various task forces and committees formed by the Government of India, previously managed the retail and digital banking portfolio at SBI.

Holding a Bachelor of Science in Agriculture and a Certified Associate of the Indian Institute of Bankers, Mr. Setty commenced his career with SBI as a Probationary Officer in 1988.

Throughout his career spanning over three decades, he has amassed extensive experience in corporate credit, retail, digital, and international banking, including operations in developed markets.

Mr. Setty has held pivotal roles at SBI, including Deputy Managing Director – Stressed Assets Resolution Group, Chief General Manager and General Manager in Corporate Accounts Group, Deputy General Manager in Commercial Branch, Indore, and VP & Head (Syndications) in SBI, New York Branch.
